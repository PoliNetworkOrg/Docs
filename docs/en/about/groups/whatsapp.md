# WhatsApp

WhatsApp has the great advantage of being widely spread in Italy, but the great disadvantage of having few tools for moderating a chat.
For this reason, the links of our WhatsApp groups are made public at the beginning of the year, to allow easy entry by students, and subsequently revoked.

Students wishing to access the WhatsApp groups of their course must necessarily contact the admin manager of their faculty, whose contact can be found [at this link](https://polinetwork.org/learnmore/about_us/) 👈

If a course is missing from the list or some contact is not up / running, you can contact the [IT delegate](https://polinetwork.org/learnmore/about_us/) 👩‍💻
